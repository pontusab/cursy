cursy

Control Cursor
from iMessage.

Text your own number. A tiny daemon on your Mac runs the prompt through cursor-agent and texts the result back. Each thread is a persistent session — it feels like a conversation with your codebase. Runs 100% locally.

$ curl -fsSL https://cursy.dev/install | bash

How it works

1brew install pontusab/tap/cursy — or the curl line above
2cursy start — scan the QR, grant two macOS permissions
3Text yourself. That's it.
